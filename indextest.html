Welcome To RGR ACADEMY

RGR ACADEMY is one of the best and familiar entrance coaching institutes in Chennai, which is established so far for providing best of all competitive coaching to students to crack entrance exams. We conduct the classes for students in many streams such as NEET, JIPMER, AIIMS, IIT/JEE and Finance with all the facilities such as well-versed environment, classy laboratories, library etc.

Each and every year there are number of students got placed in top government and private medical, engineering and finance colleges by clearing the competitive exams from our institute. Our main aim is shine our every student in a unique way to develop their profession so we think that is our great success in this society.
